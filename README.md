# 🌿 The Journey So Far

Curiosity has been the common thread throughout my career. It has taken me from engineering connected vehicles, to working alongside executive leadership, and now to helping enterprises adopt AI at scale.

I started my career as an automotive software engineer, building and validating software for **Telematics Control Units**. That experience taught me how complex systems are designed, why reliability matters, and how seemingly small engineering decisions can have a significant impact.

As my curiosity grew, I found myself wanting to understand a different kind of system: organizations. Moving into a **Chief of Staff** role gave me a front-row seat to executive decision-making, strategy, talent transformation, and the challenge of aligning people around a common goal.

Today, I work in **Enterprise AI Transformation**, where those experiences come together. I collaborate with engineering teams, product owners, Microsoft, GitHub, and business leaders to help organizations adopt AI in ways that create measurable value.

Looking back, I don't see these as separate career moves. I see them as different perspectives on the same question:

> **How do we build systems that people trust, adopt, and genuinely benefit from?**

This GitHub is where I document that journey.
