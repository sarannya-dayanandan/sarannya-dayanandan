## 🌿 My Journey So Far

Curiosity has been the common thread throughout my career. It has taken me from engineering connected vehicles, to working alongside executive leadership, and now to helping enterprises adopt AI at scale.

I started my career as an automotive software engineer, building and validating software for **Telematics Control Units**. That experience taught me how complex systems are designed, why reliability matters, and how seemingly small engineering decisions can have a significant impact.

As my curiosity grew, I found myself wanting to understand a different kind of system: organizations. Moving into a **Chief of Staff** role gave me a front-row seat to executive decision-making, strategy, talent transformation, and the challenge of aligning people around a common goal. It completely changed how I think about technology. I stopped seeing it as just software and started seeing it as something that only creates value when people understand it, trust it, and choose to use it.

Today, I work in **Enterprise AI Transformation**, collaborating with engineering teams, technology partners, and business leaders to help organizations move from AI experimentation to meaningful adoption. Along the way, I've learned that successful transformation is rarely just a technical challenge. It's equally about people, systems, and the decisions that connect them.
